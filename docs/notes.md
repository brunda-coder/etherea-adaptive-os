# Notes

## Purpose
- Capture user thoughts, ideas, and reflections
- Serve as reference for brainstorming, problem-solving, and creativity
- Support personal growth and tracking of progress over time

## Types of Notes
- **Quick Notes**: short reminders, small ideas, one-liners
- **Detailed Notes**: in-depth thoughts, step-by-step plans, analysis
- **Project Notes**: milestones, tasks, experiments, outcomes
- **Creative Notes**: sketches, inspirations, storyboards, concepts
- **Emotional Notes**: reflections on moods, challenges, motivations

## Guidelines
- Organize notes logically with headings, lists, or bullet points
- Use clear, concise language for quick understanding
- Update notes regularly to reflect progress or new insights
- Tag notes by category for faster retrieval: #ideas, #goals, #tasks, #creative, #emotions
